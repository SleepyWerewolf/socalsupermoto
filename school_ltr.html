Skillspak FAQ

How much does it cost?

The Skillspack is $239.

What is included?

On day 1 we spend three hours one on one private training on the bike, covering essential street bike control skills such as: proper clutch, gas, brake controls, cornering set up, accident avoidence, countersteering, panic stops, etc. Day 2 includes additional practice on the bike and taking the DMV test to get your M1 license. We supply the bike, insurance, and DMV test appointment.

What are the requirements?

No riding experience is required. You only need to have your California Motorcycle Permit.

How do I get my permit?

Head down to any DMV, read the Motorcycle Handbook, and take the written test. You do not need an appointment. Note if you have not recently taken the drivers written test, the DMV may ask you to take that test as well.

Do I still have to take the DMV test, or does this bypass the test?

You still take the test at the DMV, however it is very easy and we arrange the appointment for you. All riders taking the Skillspak are guaranteed to pass, or test again for free.
